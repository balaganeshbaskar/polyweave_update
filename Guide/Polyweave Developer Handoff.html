01 · PROJECT BRIEF

What we're
building.

Polyweave is a sourcing partner between India and Europe. The website needs to communicate trust, sourcing intelligence, and calm authority — not aggressive sales energy or marketplace noise.

Site should feel

Calm. Considered. Premium. Like a B2B sourcing consultancy, not a trader and not Alibaba. Lots of whitespace. Strong typography. Restrained visuals. The handwritten/blueprint visual language is intentional — it reads as honest, hand-built, human.

Site should NOT feel

Like a product catalog. Like a generic agency template. Like a marketplace. No stock photography of "global handshakes". No flag emojis. No animated counters. No confetti success states.

Audience

European procurement leads, operations managers, and small-to-mid-sized importers — primarily in Spain, Italy, Portugal, Germany, Norway. They are price-sensitive, time-poor, and skeptical. Communication is in English.

Tech notes (developer's call)

  • STACKStatic or Next.js / Astro / WordPress — developer's call. Pages are content-light, so SSG is preferred for SEO.
  • FORMSContact + sticky rail forms post to a single endpoint. Email destination: sourcing@polyweave.net. Add anti-spam (honeypot + rate-limit minimum).
  • ANALYTICSPlausible or GA4. Track CTA clicks (send-price, book-call) as named events.
  • CMSOptional. SEO landing page copy is templated; CMS only if Polyweave wants to add product pages later.
  • PERFTarget Lighthouse ≥ 95 on mobile. No heavy hero video. Self-host fonts.

Reusable building blocks. Names match the React components in the wireframes file — keep these names if you want a clean handoff.

Top nav. Logo left (POLYWEAVE in Special Elite), four links right (Home / About / Services / Contact). Active link gets a 1.5px navy underline. Sits on top of the paper grid; bottom border is 1.5px navy.

StickyForm

308 × 298px sticky rail, fixed top-right at top: 90px; right: 24px. Three fields (email, product, current price) + primary "Send price →" CTA. Visible on Home, About, Services, all SEO pages. Hidden on Contact (the page IS the form). On mobile, collapses into a sticky bottom bar with a single "Send your price →" button that opens the contact form.

4-column footer: brand · Site · Sourcing · Contact. Links to all 5 SEO pages live under "Sourcing". See section 04.

SectionTag

Eyebrow row: "02" · "MARKET REALITY" · dashed line filling remaining width. Used at the top of every content section.

Sketch box (.box)

Generic container. Hand-drawn radius, navy border. Variants: default cream-tinted, .dashed, .navy, .sea.

Numdot

36px circle, navy border, Caveat numeral inside. Used on 4-step process cards. Color-inverts on dark cards.

Btn · primary (sea)

Sea-blue fill. Cream text. SEND YOUR PRICE → in Special Elite, 14px, 1.5px tracking, uppercase. Padding 14px 22px. Hand-drawn radius.

12 · INTERACTIONS & STATES

Quiet motion.

Animations should feel like ink settling, not screens animating. Default duration 180ms, easing cubic-bezier(.2,.6,.2,1).

  • BUTTON HOVERPrimary (sea): background darkens to #23566F. Arrow translates 3px right. No shadow lift, no scale.
  • BUTTON HOVERGhost: background fills cream #F7F4EE, border stays.
  • LINK HOVERColor shift to sea #2E6F8E. Underline thickness stays; no decoration changes.
  • INPUT FOCUSBorder color: #14233A (already navy). Add 2px sea outline at rgba(46,111,142,0.25). No glow.
  • FORM SUBMITButton: subtle disabled state during submit. On success, replace form with confirmation card. On error, inline message below failing field.
  • SECTION ENTRYOptional: 80ms fade-in + 12px translate-up as section enters viewport. Disable with prefers-reduced-motion. Ship without if in doubt.
  • PRODUCT ROW HOVERHomepage product index — entire row gets a sea-tinted background rgba(46,111,142,0.06). The "READ →" tag in the right cell shifts 3px right.
  • STICKY RAILFades in when user scrolls past the hero. Fades out before footer (last 200px of page).
  • NONo parallax. No animated counters. No confetti. No carousels. No hover tilts. No "back to top" button.
